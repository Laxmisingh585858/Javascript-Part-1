			Diffrent between unary, binary and ternary 
Unary operators: 

 required one operand. For example=negative(-), logical not(!), increment(++) and decrement(--), bitwise NOT(~)
 For Example=
let a="20";
let b=+a;
console.log(b); // 20

Binary operators:

required two operands. 
for example= Addition(+),Subtraction(-), Multiplication(*),division(/)
let a=20;
let b=30;
let c=a+b;
console.log(c);  //50

ternary operators:

requires three operands.
const num1=-25;
const checknum1= (num1>=0 )?( num1==0 ? "zero":"positive"):" negative";
console.log(checknum1);
